About Me

I specialize in molecular modeling, drug discovery, protein-protein interactions, and the application of deep learning in both materials science and biological research. My expertise includes ligand- and structure-based drug design, molecular dynamics simulations, QSAR modeling, and computational immunology. I am particularly interested in leveraging artificial intelligence to enhance predictive modeling and analysis in scientific research. Passionate about teaching, I actively engage in mentoring and lecturing on Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Artificial Intelligence, helping students and professionals explore AI-driven advancements in computational sciences. I also offer consultation services in molecular docking, molecular dynamics (MD) simulations, scientific writing, and proofreading. Open to collaborations and research opportunities.
